Frequently Asked Questions

We are Happy to Answer Them!

How do I run DriveSmart?

For now, you can run DriveSmart by downloading and running the github repository in Android Studio.

How does DriveSmart work?

When you have downloaded DriveSmart on your Android device, position yourself appropriately using the camera and click "Clibrate". DriveSmart will then automatically calibrates your resting position. If it detects that you are being distracted, such as looking down, up, or sideways while driving, it will alert you by vibrating and voicing "Distracted". The vibration will stop once you position yourself back to focusing on the road.

Is there an iOS version of DriveSmart?

As of now, DriveSmart is only available and supported on Android Devices. However, we are working hard on developing an iOS version of DriveSmart for Apple users!

Do I have to sign up to use the app?

No, you do not have to sign up to use the app. However, we are planning to create a user profile page that allows users to track the number of times they were distracted while they were driving. Points are rewarded to minimum times of distracted driving.
